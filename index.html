Our mission is to empower smallholder farmers, revolutionize agriculture in Africa by combining traditional knowledge with the latest technological advances.
We aspire to improve food production by skilling farmers and raising their standard of living, and contribute to a sustainable food system.

How do we deliver impact?

We are challenging the status quo by developing and deploying accessible digital agritech tools that provide smallholder farmers with valuable insights, enhancing their knowledge, skills, and livelihoods.

Our platform bridges the gap between farmers and other food stakeholders, eliminating barriers across sectors and improving productivity and livelihoods - all at the farmers' fingertips.

Our farmers' benefits

Healthcare support

Guarantees availability and access to essential healthcare services, ensuring that everyone receives necessary medical care and support when needed.

Pre-financing arrangements

Provides the necessary financial capital required to manage the daily operational expenses of a business, ensuring smooth and uninterrupted operations.

Administrative and legal support

Provides guidance on compliance, documentation, and legal rights to ensure their operations are efficient, secure, and aligned with regulatory standards.

Children’s education

Educational opportunities for children by providing them access to quality learning resources, mentorship, and supportive learning environments for growth.​

About us

“We are a fourth-generation family-owned social enterprise, led by brothers Sam and Adam, dedicated to improving smallholder farmers’ livelihoods in Africa. By blending age-old traditions with modern technology, we create sustainable agricultural solutions.
For over 80 years, our family has produced and traded food across the region. Today, with the agriculture sector facing multiple challenges, we are committed to using innovative technology to drive meaningful change and secure a sustainable future for African agriculture.”
